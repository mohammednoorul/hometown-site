VISIT VELLOREu

Vellore has a very interesting History of its own. A strategically located town, it is well connected by Rail and bus routes to major towns of the neighboring states like A.P. Karnataka and Kerala.

Top three activities to do at Vellore

Visit Vellore fort

Built by Bommi and Thimma Reddy in the 16th century, Vellore Fort is an architectural beauty that is popularly known as the ‘Pride of Vellore and visiting the fort is one of the best things to do in the city’

Picnic at the park

Occupying over 25 hectares of area, Amirthi Zoological was opened to the public in 1967. With over 20 species of animals, visiting Amirthi Zoological Park is one of the best things to do in the city

Go to the observatory

Also known as the Kavalur Observatory, visiting the place is one of the fun things to do in Vellore. M.K. Vainu Bappu, director of Kodaikanal Observatory, established the observatory in 1986
